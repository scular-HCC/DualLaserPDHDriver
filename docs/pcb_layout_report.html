Zone descriptions

Zone A

Power Supply Strip

73 total59 SMD14 THT
Full-width strip across the top of the board (Y = 0–35 mm). All primary LDO regulator chains: ±15V (TPS7A39 + LT3042 + LT3094), 5 V reference (LM7808 → LT3042 → LTC6655 → OPA192 buffer), +5 V laser (LM7812 → LM7808 → 2× LT3045), 3.3 V (LM7812 → LM7805 → LT3045 → LP5907 1.8 V). Extensive decoupling on every sub-rail.
Key ICs: U1–U17 · U9 TPS7A39 · U3 LTC6655 · U7/U16/U17 LT3045
Zone B

Mains PSU & Bulk Caps

25 total14 SMD11 THT
Left column (X = 0–50 mm, Y = 35–200 mm). AC mains path: F1 fuse, T1 transformer header, D1 ABS10 bridge rectifier. Soft-start: Q1 IRF530 N-ch + Q2 IRF9530 P-ch gate control with D2/D3 zener clamps. C3/C4 2×2200 µF and C5/C6 2×1000 µF large THT electrolytic caps for ±18 V rails.
Key parts: D1 · Q1/Q2 · F1 · T1 · C3–C6
Zone C

±6V TEC Supply

39 total24 SMD15 THT
Second column (X = 50–110 mm, Y = 35–200 mm). D7 KBPC1501W through-hole bridge rectifier. Q3/Q4 control gate MOSFETs. Q5/Q6 MJL21193/21194 TO-264 bipolar power transistors. U12/U13 LM4040 shunt references. C35–C48: 12 large bulk electrolytic caps (up to 2200 µF). Bias, bleed, and filter resistors R20–R33.
Key parts: D7 · Q3–Q6 · U12/U13 · C35–C48 (12 caps)
Zone D

Laser & TEC Analogue Control

80 total80 SMD0 THT
Centre block (X = 110–210 mm, Y = 35–120 mm). Fully SMD. Dual-channel laser drivers: U23/U24 OPA192, U30 74AHCT125 level shifter, U31/U33 AD9833 DDS dither injection, U32 AD5064 16-bit DAC. Gate FETs Q15/Q16, BAT54 clamps, Kelvin sense Rs, inrush limiters. TEC H-bridge control: U18–U20 OPA192, U21/U22 OPA551U drivers, BZX384 gate clamps, precision voltage dividers.
Key ICs: U18–U24 · U30–U33 · Q15/Q16
Zone E

Readback

42 total40 SMD2 THT
Right-centre block (X = 210–285 mm, Y = 35–120 mm). U25–U28: 4× INA826 instrumentation amplifiers for laser and TEC current readback. U_TIA1–U_TIA6: 6× OPA380 transimpedance amplifiers for monitor photodiode (MPD) channels. U29 OPA2141 dual precision op-amp. J1/J2 BNC horizontal coax inputs. Anti-alias filters, NTC thermistor dividers, feedback and bias networks.
Key ICs: U25–U29 · U_TIA1–U_TIA6 · J1/J2 BNC
Zone F

TEC H-Bridge MOSFETs

8 total0 SMD8 THT
Lower centre (X = 110–210 mm, Y = 120–200 mm). All 8 TO-220 vertical power MOSFETs for dual-channel TEC H-bridge. Channel 1: Q7 (IRLB8721), Q8 (IRF9540N), Q9 (IRLB8721), Q10 (IRF9540N). Channel 2: Q11/Q12/Q13/Q14 same arrangement. N-ch and P-ch MOSFETs interleaved for complementary switching. Positioned close to J8 TEC output connector for short high-current loops.
Q7–Q14 · All TO-220-3 Vertical · rated ≥ 2A TEC
Zone G

Digital — Teensy / Clock / DAC

21 total19 SMD2 THT
Lower right (X = 210–285 mm, Y = 120–200 mm). A1 Teensy 4.1 MCU module (600 MHz ARM Cortex-M7, 35×18 mm). 10 MHz clock distribution chain: Y1 ASE oscillator module, U34 LMV7219M5 comparator, U35 SN74LVC1G3157 analog mux, U36 CDCE913 PLL/clock fanout. J3 BNC horizontal for external 10 MHz clock injection. Supporting decoupling and termination resistors R80–R89, C68–C71.
A1 Teensy 4.1 · U34–U36 · Y1 · J3 BNC
Zone H

DFB Laser Connectors

10 total8 SMD2 THT
Right edge strip (X = 285–300 mm). J_BTF1 and J_BTF2: 2× 2×7 2.54 mm vertical pin-header sockets for DFB1550LP butterfly laser packages. R_P1–R_P7 (Zone D): 7× 47Ω SMD series protection for J_BTF1 confirmed pins (P1/P2/P5/P10/P11/P13/P14). R90–R96: 7× 47Ω series protection for J_BTF2. All confirmed butterfly signal pins have 47Ω in-series protection. Laser reverse-bias protection: BAT54 clamps (Vr_max = 2V).
J_BTF1 · J_BTF2 · 14× 47Ω prot. (R_P1–P7, R90–R96)
