Dual-Laser PDH Lock Controller

The display is headless and read-only — the operator drives the instrument over serial. Use the console to run a sweep-to-lock on each channel: a coarse TEC/temperature scan finds the cavity mode, a fine laser-current scan walks onto the peak, then the PDH servo engages and holds the lock.

PWR ILI9341 · SPI0 · 10 Hz

This is a true-pixel 320×240 render shown at 2×. The traces are simulated: cavity transmission (channel colour, filled) during sweeps, the dispersive PDH error signal at lock-in, and the in-lock error time-series once the servo holds.
